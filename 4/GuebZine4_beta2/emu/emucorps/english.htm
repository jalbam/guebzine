If we don't do this, everything will get worse.
We are breaking the image of our big hobbye. To evade problems with big companys let actual system pass. What is EmuCorps
EmuCorps has been created to make the emulation prosperous. It's objective is join to everybody and defense the same ideal and the most reasonable sightway for it.

We defense Emulation as it is. We use it to remember those times and spend houes trying those programs that aren't buyable and that so much have meant and mean in our lives.
Thought we lean emulation from it, and we like everyonce more that new emulators exist for modern systems, we don't see this as a full part of the emulation. That everything follows it's way is very good, but if this means create problems with the companys that own those systems, when they try to defense themselves, that they place everything joint perjudicates us directly.

Nintedo had a reason to make acusations against N64 emulation. We must think that Nintendo had never mentioned GB emulation, nor NES, nor nothing. They limited to this video game system, that was reporting them benefits and they had a very good reason for their accusations.
Everything is because of those people that goes to the channels and starts asking for N64 ROMs; nobody else is culpable. Please, we can evite this and the emulation won't be seen in such a despective way. If we don't, this will start being piracy... and everything will go down.
